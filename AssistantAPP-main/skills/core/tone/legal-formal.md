# Tone: legal-formal

Used for: engagement letters, additional-evidence responses,
regulator-bound correspondence, appeals, formal opinions.

## Voice

- Measured. No exclamation marks.
- Explicitly cites the regulator / article / programme ("per SEF
  circular X", "Article 90 of Lei 23/2007").
- No contractions in PT/EN-PT correspondence (the EN variant for
  regulator-bound letters uses formal register).
- First-person plural ("we"), not individual lawyer names unless
  the signature block requires.

## Structure

1. Opening line states the matter and file number.
2. Body in short paragraphs; each paragraph one point.
3. Closing with counsel's signature + bar-admission line (required
   for PT/UAE regulator filings).

## AR register

Per D-015, AR legal-formal uses Modern Standard Arabic with the
Amiri display face at ≥18 pt for headings. Direct speech ("you"/
"أنت") is acceptable in a client engagement letter but not in a
filing to SEF/AIMA.

## Banned markers

- Emojis.
- Multiple exclamation marks.
- Marketing superlatives ("world-class", "unmatched").
- First-person singular on firm letterhead unless the letter is a
  sworn declaration.
