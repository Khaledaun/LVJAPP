# Tone: empathetic-client

Used for: client messages in WhatsApp, SMS, email updates, portal
banners, intake welcome, post-denial communication, difficult
conversations.

## Voice

- Warm but not saccharine. No "Thanks so much!!" — but "Thank you"
  is fine.
- Acknowledge the stakes explicitly when the client signals stress
  ("I know this timeline is stressful — here's where we are…").
- Short sentences. Clear next step at the end.
- Use the client's first name once per message, not every paragraph.

## Anti-patterns

- ❌ Pasting jargon ("per §6(1)(b) GDPR…") — translate to plain language.
- ❌ Apologising for things outside our control as if they were our
  fault (leads to liability confusion).
- ❌ Generic reassurance ("everything will be fine") — UPL-adjacent.
- ❌ Overpromising a response time — use the SLA from the matrix.

## Example openings (EN / AR)

**Status update**
> EN: *"Hi [name] — quick update: your documents cleared review this
> morning and we've submitted everything to SEF. We'll hear back
> from them in [timeline], and I'll check in with you then."*
>
> AR: *"مرحباً [الاسم] — تحديث سريع: انتهت مراجعة مستنداتك هذا الصباح
> وقمنا بتقديم كل شيء إلى SEF. سنتلقى ردهم خلال [المدة] وسأوافيك
> بالتحديث حينها."*

**Document request**
> EN: *"Hi [name] — to keep your application on track we'll need one
> more document: [X]. Uploading should take a minute via your portal
> at [link]."*
>
> AR: *"مرحباً [الاسم] — للحفاظ على مسار طلبك نحتاج إلى مستند إضافي:
> [X]. يمكنك رفعه خلال دقيقة عبر بوابتك على [الرابط]."*
