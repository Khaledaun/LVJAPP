# Portugal Immigration

Portuguese-law knowledge base for the destination jurisdiction `PT`.
Every article in this skill is **pending review by a Portuguese-licensed
lawyer** before flipping `confidence: authoritative`.

## Scope

- **Regulator:** SEF / AIMA. Portal flows, document chain-of-custody,
  typical timelines per visa.
- **Visa types:** D1 (work), D2 (entrepreneur), D3 (highly qualified
  activity), D7 (passive income / retiree), D8 (digital nomad),
  **Golden Visa**, **Startup Visa**, **Family Reunification**.
- **Conduct:** Ordem dos Advogados — confidentiality, advertising
  rules, UPL boundaries. Article 100 fee-sharing constraints (impacts
  LVJ commission model — cross-ref D-007).
- **Trust accounting:** Portuguese Client Trust Account (CTA)
  equivalent of IOLTA.
- **Data:** EU GDPR. DPA per tenant + per provider, lawful-basis
  tracking, right-to-erasure, DSAR (Art. 15) workflow.
- **Deadline engine inputs:** SEF/AIMA statutory timelines, Schengen
  stay rules, EU residence permit expiries.
