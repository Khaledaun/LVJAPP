# SEO · AEO+AIO · GEO

Search-discipline knowledge base for drafting LVJ content that lands on
Google SERPs, Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT / Perplexity / Claude /
Gemini citations. Covers structured data, E-E-A-T signals, and
citation-friendly content shape.

## Scope

- **SEO.** Keyword targeting per visa type, internal linking, meta tags,
  XML sitemap, Webflow-native canonical handling.
- **AEO + AIO.** FAQ schema, HowTo schema, table-formatted answers,
  Google AI Overviews formatting conventions.
- **GEO.** E-E-A-T signals (named lawyer attribution, jurisdiction
  disclaimer, citations to SEF/AIMA + EU statutes), structured data,
  list/quote-friendly formatting for LLM citation surfaces.
- **LLMO.** Per-LLM citation monitoring patterns → `MarketingMetric`
  rows.
